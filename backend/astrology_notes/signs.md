# Zodiac Sign Characteristics and Elements

## Aries (Mesha)
* **Element**: Fire | **Modality**: Cardinal | **Ruling Planet**: Mars
* **Traits**: Brave, energetic, willful, pioneering, impatient, impulsive, enthusiastic.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents the spark of life and new beginnings. It is action-oriented and thrives on challenge and competition, but can struggle with anger and impatience.

## Taurus (Vrishabha)
* **Element**: Earth | **Modality**: Fixed | **Ruling Planet**: Venus
* **Traits**: Patient, reliable, determined, artistic, stubborn, possessive, pleasure-loving.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents stability, material comfort, and appreciation of physical beauty and nature. It is slow to change but highly loyal and productive.

## Gemini (Mithuna)
* **Element**: Air | **Modality**: Mutable | **Ruling Planet**: Mercury
* **Traits**: Adaptable, intellectual, talkative, witty, restless, inconsistent, superficial.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents curiosity, communication, dual perspectives, and local networks. It seeks variety and mental stimulation but can be scattered.

## Cancer (Karka)
* **Element**: Water | **Modality**: Cardinal | **Ruling Planet**: Moon
* **Traits**: Intuitive, emotional, protective, nurturing, moody, defensive, clingy.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents emotional safety, mother, home, and deep memory. It is highly protective of its inner world and family, acting with strong instinct.

## Leo (Simha)
* **Element**: Fire | **Modality**: Fixed | **Ruling Planet**: Sun
* **Traits**: Generous, creative, proud, loyal, dramatic, egotistical, warm-hearted.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents self-expression, leadership, drama, and play. It rules the heart and seeks to shine, create, and be appreciated by others.

## Virgo (Kanya)
* **Element**: Earth | **Modality**: Mutable | **Ruling Planet**: Mercury
* **Traits**: Analytical, helpful, precise, reliable, critical, anxious, detail-oriented.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents service, health, hygiene, and analytical improvement. It seeks perfection, organization, and practicality, often struggling with worry.

## Libra (Tula)
* **Element**: Air | **Modality**: Cardinal | **Ruling Planet**: Venus
* **Traits**: Diplomatic, artistic, social, fair, indecisive, people-pleasing, romantic.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents harmony, balance, beauty, and partnerships. It seeks justice and relational connection, but can lose itself in trying to please others.

## Scorpio (Vrishchika)
* **Element**: Water | **Modality**: Fixed | **Ruling Planet**: Mars/Pluto
* **Traits**: Intense, passionate, investigative, loyal, secretive, jealous, transformative.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents deep emotional depth, secrets, shared power, regeneration, and psychological search. It holds immense power for self-transformation.

## Sagittarius (Dhanu)
* **Element**: Fire | **Modality**: Mutable | **Ruling Planet**: Jupiter
* **Traits**: Optimistic, philosophical, generous, freedom-loving, blunt, impatient, adventurous.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents search for truth, exploration, higher wisdom, and optimism. It seeks freedom of movement and belief, but can be dogmatic.

## Capricorn (Makara)
* **Element**: Earth | **Modality**: Cardinal | **Ruling Planet**: Saturn
* **Traits**: Disciplined, ambitious, practical, patient, cold, pessimistic, workaholic.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents structure, duty, social hierarchy, career accomplishments, and dry realism. It climbs the mountain of life through patience and effort.

## Aquarius (Kumbha)
* **Element**: Air | **Modality**: Fixed | **Ruling Planet**: Saturn/Uranus
* **Traits**: Humanitarian, independent, intellectual, original, detached, stubborn, rebellious.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents group networks, collective goals, innovation, science, and social progress. It seeks intellectual independence and global brotherhood.

## Pisces (Meena)
* **Element**: Water | **Modality**: Mutable | **Ruling Planet**: Jupiter/Neptune
* **Traits**: Compassionate, artistic, intuitive, spiritual, escapist, sensitive, vague.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents the ocean of consciousness, dream life, spiritual retreat, compassion, and surrender. It dissolves boundaries to connect with the divine.
