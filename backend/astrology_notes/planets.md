# Planetary Significations and Meanings

## The Sun (Surya)
* **Core Energy**: Soul, self, ego, vitality, authority, leadership, father, identity, creative spark.
* **Keywords**: Radiance, purpose, essence, pride, consciousness, will-power.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents the core self and how one shines in the world. A strong Sun gives confidence, leadership, and vitality, while a challenged Sun can lead to pride, ego issues, or lack of confidence.

## The Moon (Chandra)
* **Core Energy**: Mind, emotions, intuition, nurturing, mother, subconscious, feelings, security.
* **Keywords**: Receptivity, instinct, reflection, fluctuations, comfort, memory.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents the emotional nature and how one feels secure. It governs the subconscious mind, emotional reactions, and our capacity to nurture and be nurtured.

## Mercury (Budha)
* **Core Energy**: Intellect, communication, logic, analysis, learning, commerce, speech, wit.
* **Keywords**: Mentality, dexterity, expression, curiosity, flexibility, detail.
* **Astrological Signification**: Governs how we think, process information, speak, and write. It is the planet of trade, commerce, and logical reasoning.

## Venus (Shukra)
* **Core Energy**: Love, relationships, beauty, harmony, art, finance, luxury, attraction, values.
* **Keywords**: Affection, union, appreciation, style, pleasure, relationship.
* **Astrological Signification**: Governs our capacity for love, our relationship patterns, aesthetic taste, and our attitude towards wealth and luxury.

## Mars (Mangala)
* **Core Energy**: Action, energy, drive, courage, passion, ambition, conflict, physical strength, anger.
* **Keywords**: Impulse, assertion, desire, initiative, combat, momentum.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents our drive, physical energy, and how we assert ourselves. It rules courage and the ability to overcome obstacles, but can also manifest as anger, impulsiveness, and conflict.

## Jupiter (Guru)
* **Core Energy**: Wisdom, expansion, abundance, luck, philosophy, higher education, spirituality, optimism.
* **Keywords**: Growth, generosity, belief, faith, guidance, fortune.
* **Astrological Signification**: The great benefic, representing wisdom, spiritual teachings, expansion, and good luck. It guides us toward search for meaning and truth.

## Saturn (Shani)
* **Core Energy**: Discipline, structure, responsibility, delay, restriction, karma, time, boundaries, maturity.
* **Keywords**: Limitation, structure, duty, endurance, structure, focus.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents trials, delays, and life lessons. It is the teacher that brings order, discipline, and karmic justice, forcing us to mature through patience and hard work.

## Rahu (North Node)
* **Core Energy**: Obsession, worldly desires, future direction, ambition, illusion, innovation, unusual paths.
* **Keywords**: Focus, expansion, craving, destiny, disruption, technology.
* **Astrological Signification**: In Vedic astrology, Rahu represents the path of future growth, where we have intense desires and lessons to learn. It signifies where we must expand, often through unconventional means.

## Ketu (South Node)
* **Core Energy**: Detachment, spirituality, past life talents, liberation, isolation, inner wisdom.
* **Keywords**: Release, mastery, renunciation, psychic ability, completion.
* **Astrological Signification**: Represents past life karma, skills we have already mastered, and areas where we feel detached or seek spiritual liberation (Moksha). It urges us to let go of material attachment.
